This roast beef snadwhich never fails to impress, and take care of our late night cravings.

Ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  2. 1 medium onion, sliced
  3. 1 container (12 ounces) McCormick Simply Better Beef Gravy
  4. 1/2 pound thinly sliced roast beef
  5. 4 slices Italian bread, (cut 1 to 1 1/4-inch thick)
  6. 4 slices provolone cheese

    7. Instructions

    Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat.
    Add onion; cook and stir 4 to 5 minutes or until tender.
    Add gravy and roast beef; cook until heated through, stirring occasionally.
